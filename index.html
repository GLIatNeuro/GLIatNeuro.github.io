The Green Labs Initiative

The benefits of scientific research are often accompanied by high environmental costs and heavy reliance on single-use plastics, hazardous chemicals, and large equipment. The Green Labs Initiative at the Montreal Neurological Institute and Hospital (The Neuro) strives to address these issues through sustainability projects and community outreach programs.
